WHY ROME?

Rome, the Eternal City

A city where ancient history and contemporary energy coalesce surrounded by seven hills, Rome stands as a testament to the grandeur of the past and the vivacity of the present.

Wander through the iconic Colosseum, where echoes of gladiator battles still resonate, or stroll along the cobblestone streets to discover hidden trattorias serving delectable Roman cuisine. Adorned with architectural marvels like the Pantheon and Vatican City, Rome is a living museum, a canvas painted with the brushstrokes of emperors and artists alike. Indulge in the charm of its piazzas, savor authentic gelato, and immerse yourself in the eternal charm of the Eternal City, where every step is a journey through time.

FOOD

What and where to eat in Rome

Pasta

I can't pick just one, they are all delicious: L’Amatriciana, La Carbonara, La cacio e pepe.

Trecca - Roma

Address: Via Alessandro Severo 220, Ostiense

What is it?

All the recipies use simple ingredients, but the combination of flavours is just amazing.

Saltimbocca

A simple second dish made with high quality ingredients that would surprise you with its taste.

Saltimbocca Ristorante

Address: Via di Tor Millina 5, Piazza Navona

What is it?

It is a calf veal filet, with a slice of sweet prosciutto crudo, sautéed in butter with white wine and sage.

Maritozzo

This pastry is the symbol of Rome and the best part is that you can enjoy it all day long, with a coffee in the morning or in the afternoon.

Il Maritozzaro

Address: Via Ettore Rolli 50, Trastevere

What is it?

A maritozzo is a traditional sweet bread roll, filled with fluffy panna – like whipped cream.
